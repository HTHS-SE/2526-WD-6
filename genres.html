Sci-fi Subgenres

As one of the largest genres, Sci-Fi has a lot of subgenres, including:

  • Apocalyptic/Post Apocalyptic
  • Biopunk
  • Cyberpunk
  • Dystopian
  • Sci-fi Horror (Frankenstein, Jekyll and Hyde, etc)
  • Time Travel

Apocalyptic and Post-apocalyptic

This subgenre focuses on a world during or after an apocalypse, or on characters attempting to prevent the same. Sources of apocalypses vary widely, from climate to medical to religious. Modern technology is often described as failing, forcing characters to explore the world in a completely new way.
